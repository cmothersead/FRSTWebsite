Challenger is our competitive group for athletes from 11 to 14 years old. This group aims to push athletes forward from good to great! Athletes in this group are held to high standards of attendance, achievement, and preparation as they learn the physical, mental, and emotional skills necessary to excel in the sport. To qualify for this group, athletes must have multiple Divisional cuts, and are expected to attend all meets.

Requirements

  • Attendance
    • No practice attendance requirement
    • Attend all Required meets
  • Performance
      Athletes must be
    • Water safe
    • Comfortable with swimming multiple lengths of the pool without stopping
