It is our goal to become the most innovative, athlete-centered program in our state. With an “athlete-centered” goal in place, our coaches have designed a system to make our athletes successful at all levels within our sport. Athletes will receive instruction, as well as training, based specifically on their needs as an athlete. With long-term success always in mind, FRST will offer an excellent experience for all ages of our athletes. Outstanding achievement and vast improvement will always be our priority.
