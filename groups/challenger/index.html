Challenger is our competitive group for athletes from 11 to 14 years old. This group aims to push athletes forward from good to great! Athletes in this group are held to high standards of attendance, achievement, and preparation as they learn the physical, mental, and emotional skills necessary to excel in the sport.

These athletes are commonly seen competing at the Divisional and State championships, as well as representing FRST wherever competition takes us. As members of Challenger carve out their place in the sport they will experience many opportunities to experience the fun and challenge the sport has to offer on a local, state, and national stage.

To qualify for this group, athletes must have multiple Divisional cuts for age 12 of their respective gender and be ready to pursue higher standards. Practice attendance is a high priority, and athletes are expected to attend all meets.

Requirements

Attendance

  • Attend all Required meets
  • 80% Practice Attendance

    • "The greatest ability is availability."

Performance

    Athletes must be
  • Divisional qualified in 3 or more events
  • IMR qualified
    • And are expected to be faster than
  • 100 Free 1:10SCY/1:20LCM

Training

    Athletes must be able to hold
  • 1:30SCY/1:40LCM Freestyle
  • 2:00SCY/2:10LCM Kick
    • without difficulty

Attitude

  • Bring a positive, growth-minded attitude to practice every day
  • Be excited to compete and improve as often as possible
