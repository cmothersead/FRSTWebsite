Endeavour is our developmental group for athletes 11 to 14 years old. The primary goals of this group are to create a fun and competive environment where athletes learn to love the sport and dive in to the skills and sacrifices it takes to become a championship swimmer!

Athletes in this group will be exposed to teaching, training, and a fantastic group of teammates with which to create friendships and bonds that go beyond the pool. Endeavour works to prepare athletes for the rigors that lie ahead in the higher levels, while providing a sense of purpose for competition, struggle, and sacrifice.

This group is a springboard for great achievement that shows that our greatness is only limited by our willingness to strive for it!

Requirements

Attendance

  • 80% Practice Attendance
  • Attend all Required meets

    • "The greatest ability is availability."

Performance

    Athletes must be
  • Competitively legal in all 4 strokes
  • Faster than at least one 10&Under Divisional cut
    • And are expected to be faster than
  • 100 Free 1:20SCY/1:30LCM

Training

    Athletes must be able to hold
  • 1:40SCY/1:50LCM Freestyle
  • 2:10SCY/2:20LCM Kick
    • without difficulty
