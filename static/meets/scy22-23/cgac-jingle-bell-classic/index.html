A local meet for our young up-and-comers. This meet provides great competition at a very nice local venue. It provides a great opportunity for those who have worked hard through the fall to break out and show their improvement!

GROUPS

  • Spark
  • Ignition
  • Launch
  • Discovery
  • Endeavour
  • Challenger
  • Exploration
  • Eclipse
  • All Senior Groups

INFORMATION

This meet is Timed Final format and features many local teams from Johnson County and the surrounding areas.

If you have any questions about the meet, please ask your group lead coach for information!
