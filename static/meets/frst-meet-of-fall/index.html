"Get the season started right at home!"

The first meet of the fall season comes at our home pool with a fun and interesting twist on club meets. Events are swum by odd-ages (9 & Under, 10-11, 12-13, 14 & Over) offering opportunities for kids to compete up or down from where they will see the rest of the season.

All swimmers are required to attend, with the exceptions of swimmers beginning their first season with FRST! It is a great experience and packed with fun, competition, and fast swimming!

INFORMATION

As a home meet, participation and volunteering from all members of the club is ESSENTIAL to the meet's success. Due to the attendance requirement, athletes are automatically committed to attend the meet.

If you have any questions about the meet, please ask your group lead coach for information!
