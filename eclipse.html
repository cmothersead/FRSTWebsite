Colin Mothersead

Eclipse is our high performance group for athletes from 11 to 14 years old. This group is for athletes who represent FRST at the state, zone, and national levels. Eclipse provides the most rigor and accountability of any FRST age group. Athletes from this group are expected to be highly committed to their swimming success and serve as examples for their teammates and peers. Members of Eclipse often go on to see great success in high school and college swimming as a part of FRST and beyond.

Requirements

  • Attendance
    • 90% Practice Attendance
    • 100% Meet Attendance
  • Performance
      Athletes must be
    • Age Group State qualified
    • IMX qualified
      • And are expected to be faster than
    • 100 Free 1:00SCY/1:10LCM
  • Training
      Athletes must be able to hold
    • 1:20SCY/1:30LCM Freestyle
    • 1:50SCY/2:00LCM Kick
      • without difficulty
  • Attitude
      Athletes must
    • Bring a positive, growth-minded attitude to practice every day
    • Be willing to take on leadership responsibilites within the 11-14 age group
    • Be capable of holding themselves and teammates accountable in a critical, but positive way
