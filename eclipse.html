Group Overview

Eclipse is FRST's highest performing 14&Under group. Eclipse athletes represent FRST at Indiana's Age Group State and beyond. Eclipse members are leaders within the team and are expected to be engaged not only in their own success, but also in the success of the rest of their teammates. In order to qualify for Eclipse, swimmers must meet the minimum qualifications for all previous groups, as well as the requirements listed below:

Group Requirements

  • Attendance
    • 90% Practice Attendance
    • 100% Meet Attendance
  • Performance
      Athletes must be
    • Age Group State qualified
    • IMX qualified
      • And are expected to be faster than
    • 100 Free 1:00Y/1:10L
  • Training
      Athletes must be able to hold
    • 1:20Y/1:30L Freestyle
    • 1:50Y/2:00L Kick
      • without difficulty
  • Attitude
      Athletes must
    • Bring a positive, growth-minded attitude to practice every day
    • Be willing to take on leadership responsibilites within the 11-14 age group
    • Be capable of holding themselves and teammates accountable in a critical, but positive way
