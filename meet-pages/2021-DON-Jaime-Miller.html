INFORMATION:
We will be celebrating the end of school for the summer by heading outdoors to Columbus for Donner's Jaime Miller Invite. This is a great pool where Indiana swimming has hosted several State and Zone meets. We get to swim fast while enjoying some sun at this fun mid-season meet! Remember to pack sunscreen!

GOALS:
We'll have seen some serious competition already, by this point in the season. From here we aim to improve specific technical issues and racing strategy while we move forward into the second half of the season.
