2021 Fishers Area Swimming Tigers 500 Invitational

DATES:
May 14-16, 2021

ENTRY DEADLINE:
April 23, 2021

GROUPS:

  • Challenger
  • Discovery
  • Exploration
  • Launch (Recommended)

INFORMATION:
For our first major meet of the summer we will be travelling to the north side of Indy to Fishers High School to compete against some very strong competition. No word yet on what COVID protocols will look like at this point in the season, but we have gone to Fishers to swim fast many times in the past and have always come away with impressive performances!

GOALS:
Coming out of our dual meets and home meet, we will have had plenty of exposure to racing in the long course pool. With the novelty of it fading, we are looking to post some fast swims and punch as many tickets to Divisionals and State as we can at this early point in the season.
