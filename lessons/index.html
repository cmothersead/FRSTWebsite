Learn to Swim with the FRST Swim School!

Our mission for our swim lessons program is quite simple. We're here to TEACH children HOW TO SWIM and BE SAFE around the water. Our professionally trained instructors pride themselves on providing quality lessons that are fun and rewarding so that children learn to love and respect the water as much as we do at FRST. Students in our programs will progressively learn a variety of skills that will allow them to be safe and comfortable in any aquatic environment.
